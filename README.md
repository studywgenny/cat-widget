### ⋆˚꩜｡ what is this???

a tiny desktop companion to keep you company
while you study or code or overthink ✩

### ˖˚⊹ ꣑ৎ what she does

- she can roam around on your screen, she can fade out and hop somewhere else, and then fade back in!
- she's draggable, because sometimes she can spawn on text you want to read, not to worry!

### ₊˚꩜ getting started

**one-time setup** *(only ever do this once)*

1. install [Node.js](https://nodejs.org) ~ grab the **LTS** version and click
   through the installer
2. make sure your folder has everything inside it: `main.js`, `preload.js`,
   `kitty.html`, `package.json`, and an `assets` folder with your `kitty.gif`
   as it is an asset. you can replace kitty.gif with any gif of your choice!
   the link to the gif is [here](https://uk.pinterest.com/pin/883761126868578713/).
4. open the folder, click the address bar at the top, type `cmd`, press enter
    a little black terminal opens
5. type `npm install` and press enter ~ this grabs electron (takes a minute!)

**running her** *(every time)*

1. open the folder & pop open the terminal again (`cmd` in the address bar)
2. type `npm start` and press enter
3. she appears !! ♡

to close her ~ just close the terminal window, or press `Ctrl + C` inside it
